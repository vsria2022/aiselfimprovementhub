Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how the Shubh Katha desktop creator tool (the "Software"), operated by the individual publisher of the Shubh Katha channels (the "Developer"), handles data in connection with its integrations with third-party platforms, including TikTok.

The Software is a single-operator tool. It has no hosted user accounts, collects no data from any person other than the Developer, and does not share data with any third party except the platform APIs it integrates with, as described below.

2. Data the Software Handles

To publish videos to, and retrieve statistics about, the Developer’s own social media accounts, the Software handles a small amount of data belonging exclusively to the Developer:

  • OAuth credentials issued by platform APIs (for example, TikTok access and refresh tokens), authorising the Software to act on the Developer’s own account.
  • Basic profile information returned by those APIs (for example, the Developer’s open_id, display name, and avatar URL, where applicable).
  • Metadata and performance statistics for videos published by the Developer (for example, video ID, title, view count, like count, comment count).
  • Video files and accompanying metadata (titles, descriptions, tags, thumbnails) produced by the Developer for upload to the Developer’s own account.

3. Where This Data Lives

All credentials, tokens, and platform statistics handled by the Software are stored locally on the Developer’s own computer. The Software does not operate a server, does not transmit this data to any system operated by the Developer other than the laptop on which the Software runs, and does not share it with any third party.

4. Third-Party Platforms

The Software communicates directly with platform APIs (including TikTok) in order to carry out the Developer’s instructions (for example, uploading a video or reading a stats dashboard). Those platforms handle data according to their own privacy policies:

  • TikTok: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • TikTok for Developers: developers.tiktok.com/doc/privacy-policy

The Developer’s use of those platforms is governed by those platforms’ own terms and policies; this Policy does not replace them.

5. Data the Software Does Not Collect

The Software does not:

  • Collect data about any person other than the Developer;
  • Collect data about followers, fans, or viewers of the Developer’s content (beyond aggregated stats that the platform surfaces to the Developer’s own dashboard);
  • Sell, lease, or otherwise disclose any data to third parties for advertising, profiling, or any other purpose;
  • Use cookies, fingerprinting, analytics beacons, or any other tracking technology directed at third parties.

6. Retention and Deletion

Because data is stored locally on the Developer’s own computer, the Developer can delete any stored credentials or statistics at any time by removing the Software’s configuration directory. Platform access can also be revoked at any time from within each platform’s connected-apps settings (for example, in TikTok’s Settings and Privacy > Security and Permissions > Manage app permissions).

7. Security

The Developer takes reasonable steps to protect credentials on the local machine, including storing tokens in standard operating-system credential locations and keeping the machine itself under up-to-date operating system and disk-encryption settings. No method of electronic storage is perfectly secure, and the Developer cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

The Software is not directed at, and does not knowingly collect information from, children under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact
