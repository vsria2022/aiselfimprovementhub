Shubh Katha

Stories from Hindu mythology, one auspicious day at a time.

Shubh Katha is an independent desktop creator tool used by a single publisher to produce and share short-form Hindu mythology videos on social platforms including TikTok and YouTube. The tool runs locally on the creator’s own computer and does not offer public accounts, user-generated content, or any service directly exposed to third parties.
